Rejuvenation Route  -（35yrs+）
Rejuvenation routes will be aimed at a more middle aged and elderly age group 
and be orientated around providing visitors with a space in which to find respite 
in the urban environment. These routes will entail:
• Picturesque, peaceful walks
• Pampering and wellness facilities
• Yoga, tai chi, and square-dancing spaces
• Health food outlets
• Meditation spaces
• Therapeutic shopping outlets
• Areas for spiritual engagement (i.e. temples) and reflection
